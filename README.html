2P Games

15 multiplayer games — play locally or online. No ads, no accounts.

Overview

2P Games is a collection of 15 touch-friendly multiplayer games for two players. Play locally on a single device (left/right or top/bottom split) or online with a friend via room codes and quick match. Built as a Capacitor wrapper around an HTML5/JavaScript game engine; online multiplayer is brokered through Firebase.

Features

  • 15 games in one app, all local 2-player
  • 10 games support online multiplayer via room codes or quick match: 4 in a Row, Caro, Awalé, Memory, Duck-Day Chess, Tank Wars, Ship Battle, Dots & Boxes, Word Clash, Tic Tac Toe
  • Local-only titles: Tennis (with power-ups), Air Hockey, Mini Golf, Star Clash, Wheel of Funktune, Horse Jump
  • Built-in instructions for every game (? button)
  • Touch-optimised controls split between halves of the screen
  • Calming background music with a toggle
  • Rematch support in every game
  • No ads, no tracking, no in-app purchases

Build & install

Capacitor (Android) wrapping a vanilla HTML/JS web app under www/.

npm install
npx cap sync android
cd android && ./gradlew assembleRelease
