2P Games is a collection of 16 touch-friendly local multiplayer games designed
for two players sharing one device. No internet needed, no accounts, no ads.

GAMES:

- Tennis — Classic pong with funny power-ups (ghost ball, magnet, quake!)
- 4 in a Row — Drop discs, connect four to win
- Pool — Realistic 2D pool with proper physics
- Memory — Flip cards and match pairs
- Snakes — Two snakes, swipe to steer, don't crash
- Air Hockey — Flick the puck into the opponent's goal
- Tank Wars — Artillery duels on destructible terrain
- Ship Battle — Classic battleship with drag-to-place ships
- Mini Golf — Take turns putting through tricky courses
- Star Clash — Space invaders with two ships and control zones
- Munch Maze — 2-player maze chase with dots, monsters, and power pellets
- Awalé — Ancient African seed-sowing strategy game
- Mastermonde — Crack the color code in fewest guesses
- Wheel of Funktune — Spin the wheel, guess the phrase
- Pixel Run — Split-screen auto-runner with coins and enemies
- Horse Jump — Split-screen horse racing over hurdles

All games feature intuitive touch controls split between the two halves of the
screen (left/right or top/bottom). Built-in calming background music with
toggle. Every game has rematch support.

Free and open source. No ads. No tracking. No in-app purchases.
